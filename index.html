Who are we and what are we doing ?

Audencia Junior Conseil is a professional organization under the French law of 1901, whose objective is to allow students to apply the educational teachings provided by their school. Audencia Junior Conseil assists companies of all sizes and in all sectors in their development, by providing them with the newly acquired skills of students specialized in the relevant fields.
